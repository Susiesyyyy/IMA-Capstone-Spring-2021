The Silk Road
is known as a famous trade road.

But was this "trade" road really about trade?

The Silk Road was named by the German geographer and traveler,
Ferdinand von Richthofen, in 1877 CE.

Is it a PROPER name?

What was the route's original purpose?

What was the route's actual function?

Starting from Qin Dynasty, Xiongnu, a nomandic community near ancient China,

began invading the Central Plains for foods and resourses.

Since they were skillful in fighting in the border area,

ancient China can hardly defend its territory.

Han Dynasty wanted to make an alliance against Xiongnu.

The emperor dispatched the government official Zhang Qian to explore the surrounding areas and find other countries with which to form alliances.

Zhang Qian found Yuezhi (月氏) first and learned that they had also been experiencing invasions from Xiongnu.

In the years that followed, the Han government found other countries like Yuezhi.

Han led the formation of an alliance with these countries (宗主国).

The other nations in this alliance (subsidary countries) gave the Han government treasures, horses, and jade.

In exchange, the Han government gave them Silk.

The Han troops also used Silk as a millitary currency when they traveled the Silk Road.

The Han government also set up stations in the other countries and dispatched officials to manage them.

Many other cultural artifacts, like paper, languages, and buddhism, were also exchanged along the Silk Road.

The original purpose of the Silk Road was

to connect the allied countries,

allowing for millitary and diplomatic travel.

Trade is the by-product of the Silk Road.

The Belt and Road Initiative

is known as One Belt One Road (一带一路).

It is a global infrastructure development strategy developed by the Chinese government in 2013.

In the news and official documents, the Belt and Road Initiative is usually called THE NEW SILK ROAD.

Does it mean that the Belt and Road Initiative is the revival of the Silk Road?

Or, it is similiar to the Silk Road?

NO

The merchandise traded on the routes are the resources

not the luxury goods as the ancient time.

The government developed basic infrastructure for transportation

instead of setting stations in the past.

Trade is the main component on the Belt and Road.

Belt and Road Initiative is quite different from the Silk Road.

It is difficult to define the original purpose of the Belt and Road now.

But it is clear that it has geo-economic and geo-political purposes.

Chinese government plans

to develop a new economic globalizaton that leads by China.

THE SILK ROAD

is not a good metaphor for both routes.

The misunderstandings may make the public ignores the essense of the routes.
